On February 15th, we visited an anime convention in Dortmund - Tadaima.

It was a funny experience, because already at the doors we discovered that it is forbidden to take photos there.

It was unexpected for sure, but didn't completely derail our plans: the weather outside was nice, even if a bit chill,
and we managed to take photos of several brave volunteers from the queue.

Inside the post, there are more photos, and all of them are downloadable in high quality!
